Hacking Health Ottawa 2017 | Shopify Building
April 28-30, 2017
Private Repository
Group of 5 programmers, we teamed with a neurosurgeon, a nurse practitioner and a registered nurse to build a solution for patients with brain tumours. The webapp showed a visual representation of the progress of the patient, included relevant information depending on the progress of the patient through their journey, allowed booking appointments and helped facilitate patient navigation on the hospital campus with a map showing the specific location of the room. The webapp can be accessed by the patient, the caregiver and the doctor. Technologies used: HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Django.
